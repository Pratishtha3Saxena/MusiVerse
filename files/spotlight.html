COLDPLAY


Coldplay are a British rock band formed in London in 1997. They consist of vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, drummer Will Champion and creative director Phil Harvey.

Over 100 million records have been sold worldwide. They have recieved 7 Grammy Awards, including Song of the Year and Record of the Year for "Clocks" and Album of the Year for "Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends".
